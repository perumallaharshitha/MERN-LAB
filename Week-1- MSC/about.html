ABOUT COLLEGE

Maris Stella College, established in 1962, is an autonomous institution managed by the Franciscan Missionaries of Mary. The college focuses on providing quality education and empowering young women to contribute positively to society. With over 2000 students and numerous academic programs across disciplines, the college continues its mission of promoting holistic development and values-based education.

Over the decades, Maris Stella College has achieved excellence in education and community development. It has earned recognition from various accrediting bodies, including the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) and the University Grants Commission (UGC). The college strives to maintain high academic standards and offers a range of programs at undergraduate and postgraduate levels, while also promoting an eco-friendly campus and social outreach programs.

VISION

Empower, Enrich, Excel, Transform

To contribute to a just and equitable society through quality education for leadership and social responsibility in an environment of academic excellence and sound values.

MISSION

The mission of the college is to empower young women through a transformative education to form intellectually competent, morally upright, socially committed, and spiritually inspired women imbued with the values of humanism in the service of society.
