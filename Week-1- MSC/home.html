National Cadet Corps (NCC) and Student Activities

At Maris Stella College, we encourage our students to participate in various extracurricular activities such as the National Cadet Corps (NCC) and student-run clubs. The NCC program provides an excellent platform for leadership, discipline, and nation-building, while student clubs offer a creative and collaborative environment for learning and growth.

Our NCC cadets participate in various activities such as drills, camps, and community service. It not only enhances physical fitness but also fosters a sense of responsibility and patriotism.

Students actively engage in cultural, technical, sports, and literary clubs. These activities help to nurture creativity, teamwork, and leadership skills, allowing students to grow beyond academics and become well-rounded individuals.
